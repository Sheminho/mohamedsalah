inception and beginning

Mohamed Salah was born on June 15, 1992 in the village of Najrij in the city of Basyoun in the Gharbia governorate. Luck did not help Salah to enroll in a large university, but rather he preferred to join the Wireless Institute because of the difficult financial conditions he lived, in addition to his love for football, and he was unable to enroll in high school The general public is in the city of Basyoun, due to his desire to stay in Cairo to join the Arab Contractors Club.


The beginning of his career with the Arab Contractors Club

Salah started his football career in the clubs of Ittihad Basyoun and Osamathon in Tanta, before joining as a junior in the Arab Contractors Club in 2006, where his first creative stage was. Mohamed Salah played his first match in the Egyptian Premier League on May 3, 2010, at the age of eighteen, against Mansoura in a match that ended in a 1-1 draw. Salah participated as a substitute in this match. In the 2010-11 season, Salah became a key player in the team, and scored his first goal on December 25, 2010 against Al-Ahly in a match that ended in a 1--1 draw. His high abilities, skills, and blazing speed made him the focus of media attention.
